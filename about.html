My Story

I am a Health Informatics graduate and licensed dental professional who bridges the gap between clinical practice and information technology. My unique journey began in dental medicine, where I gained hands-on experience managing electronic medical records, coordinating patient care, and conducting public health research. This clinical foundation sparked my passion for improving healthcare delivery through technology.

Now pursuing my Masters in Health Informatics at Indiana University, I integrate my clinical insights with advanced informatics skills to support research on health information exchange, workflow optimization, and quality improvement. My work focuses on leveraging data analytics, interoperability standards, and innovative systems to enhance patient care and research outcomes.

My Values

  • Clinical Excellence: Ensuring the highest quality in healthcare delivery through evidence-based practices and continuous improvement
  • Data Integrity: Maintaining HIPAA compliance and ethical standards while enabling research and innovation
  • Continuous Learning: Embracing new technologies and methodologies to stay at the forefront of health informatics
  • Interdisciplinary Collaboration: Working effectively with clinicians, researchers, and technologists to achieve common healthcare goals

What I Do

As a Clinical Data Analyst Intern at GTech Technologies, I lead full-stack development of AI-integrated EMR systems that enhance clinical decision support and workflow automation. I specialize in data integration using SQL, Python, and R, and have extensive experience with FHIR R4, REST APIs, and ETL pipelines. My work spans from front-end development to database optimization, always with a focus on improving patient care outcomes and research capabilities.

I am passionate about translating complex healthcare data into actionable insights that drive quality improvement initiatives and support evidence-based decision making in clinical settings.
